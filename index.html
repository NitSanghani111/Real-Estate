Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about buying, selling, and renting properties

01

How do I start the home buying process?

Starting your home buying journey is easy! First, get pre-approved for a mortgage to understand your budget. Then, contact our expert agents who will help you find properties that match your needs and guide you through viewing, negotiations, and closing. We're with you every step of the way.

02

What documents do I need to sell my property?

To sell your property, you'll need: property title deed, recent tax receipts, identity proof, sale deed of previous owner, building approval plans, occupancy certificate, and NOC from society/housing board. Our team will provide a complete checklist and help you organize all required documents.

03

How long does it take to close a real estate transaction?

Typically, a real estate transaction takes 30-45 days to close. This includes property inspection, financing approval, title search, and final paperwork. However, the timeline can vary based on financing type, property condition, and legal requirements. We work to ensure a smooth and timely closing process.

04

Do you charge any fees for property consultation?

No! Your initial consultation is completely free. We offer complimentary property valuation, market analysis, and expert advice. Our commission is only charged upon successful completion of a transaction. We believe in building trust and providing value before earning your business.

05

Can I view properties virtually before visiting?

Absolutely! We offer virtual tours, 360-degree videos, and detailed photo galleries for most of our properties. You can explore properties from the comfort of your home and shortlist your favorites before scheduling physical visits. Contact us to arrange a virtual viewing.

06

What is the difference between buying and renting?

Buying offers equity building, tax benefits, and long-term stability but requires upfront investment and maintenance responsibility. Renting provides flexibility, lower upfront costs, and no maintenance burden but builds no equity. Our advisors can help you evaluate which option aligns with your financial goals and lifestyle.

07

How do you determine property valuation?

We use a comprehensive approach including: comparative market analysis (recent sales in the area), property condition assessment, location advantages, current market trends, infrastructure development, and unique features. Our experts provide accurate valuations backed by data and market insights.

08

What areas and cities do you cover?

We have a presence in over 120 cities across the country, covering major metropolitan areas, tier-2 cities, and emerging real estate markets. Our network includes residential, commercial, and luxury properties in prime locations. Contact us to check availability in your desired area.
